Employee Handbook 2026

Paid Time Off Policy
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid time off per calendar year. PTO can be carried over up to a maximum balance of 25 days. Any balance above 25 days is forfeited at the end of the first payroll cycle in January unless an exception is approved by Human Resources.

Travel Reimbursement Policy
Employees must submit travel reimbursement requests within 30 calendar days of completing business travel. Hotel expenses require itemized receipts. Meals are reimbursable up to 65 dollars per day unless otherwise approved by the employee's department vice president.

Remote Work Expectations
Employees approved for hybrid work must attend in-person planning sessions on the first Tuesday of each month. Team leads are responsible for publishing attendance expectations at least five business days in advance.
